about myself

I have always found myself fascinated with Video Games. I could always appreciate the way you become fully immersed in an entirely different world every time you pick up a new game. I also found that these games would help me connect with people through gameplay or even just talking about our own experiences. Once I began planning an academic career beyond high school I knew I needed to make my mark and I needed to pass along these experiences to the next generation.

I am a Computer Science Major at California State University Dominguez Hills, currently in my junior year. I have been doing my best to prepare myself for a career in game design through both formal and informal education. Though I am a Computer Science major, I have taken any coursework I found to be the most resourceful for a career in game development. In my time enrolled, I have completed work a various HTML-based games of varying styles with more on the way. My coursework has included:
  • Intro to Game Design/Programming
  • Data Structures
  • Analysis of Algorithms
  • C++ Programming
  • Java Programming
With the following planned for the Spring 2020 Semester:
  • Computer Graphics
  • Advanced Game Design (game design using Unity)
  • Linear Algebra
  • Differential Equations
  • Probability and Statistics
  • Operating Systems
