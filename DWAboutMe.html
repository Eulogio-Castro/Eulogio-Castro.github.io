I have always found myself fascinated with Video Games. I could always appreciate the way you become fully immersed in an entirely different world every time you pick up a new game. I also found that these games would help me connect with people through gameplay or even just talking about our own experiences. Once I began planning an academic career beyond high school I knew I needed to make my mark and I needed to pass along these experiences to the next generation.

I am a Computer Science Major and Mathematics Minor at California State University Dominguez Hills, entering my final year. I have been doing my best to prepare myself for a career in game design through both formal and informal education. Though I am a Computer Science major, I have taken any coursework I found to be the most resourceful for a career in game development. In my time enrolled, I worked on a few projects with deadlines of only a couple of weeks each, which are playable in my portfolio section.
