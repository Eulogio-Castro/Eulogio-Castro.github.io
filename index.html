about myself

I am a Computer Science Major at California State University Dominguez Hills, currently in my junior year. In my time enrolled, I have completed work a various HTML-based games of varying styles. These include:

  • Freerunner: my very first project, a simple platformer game.
  • Dungeons of Hyrule: an action game with Platformer elements.
  • Inferno: a top-down shooter using Procedural Generation to create each level upon loading the level.
  • Spiderman: City Crisis: a mixture of all of my past projects, showcasing concepts learned throughout the year.

I am someone who picked up a controller as kid and refused to put it down, and I want nothing more than to work alongside a team to bring the same amazing experience I had to the new generation.
